## Legal & Ethical Testing Guidelines

### Allowed (User-Level Testing)

- Playback validation (start, pause, resume)
- UI/UX testing and navigation
- Ad behavior observation
- Device compatibility testing
- Performance observations (buffering, crashes)

---

### Not Allowed (Without Permission)

- Bypassing ads or subscriptions
- Intercepting or modifying network traffic
- Reverse engineering applications
- Load or stress testing production systems
- Automated scraping or intrusive automation

---

## How to Document in Portfolio

Use professional wording such as:

"Performed user-level functional and playback testing on publicly available streaming platforms to validate UI behavior, ad insertion flow, and cross-device compatibility."

---

## Best Practice

Always:
- Test as a real user
- Avoid intrusive tools on production apps
- Document observations clearly
- Focus on functional behavior and user experience
